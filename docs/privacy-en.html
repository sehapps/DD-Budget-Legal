DD Budget — Privacy Policy

Application: DD Budget
Contact: sehappdev@gmail.com

In short: DD Budget stores all of your financial data only on your device. The app uses third-party services with limited data to show ads, report crashes, measure usage, and provide optional Google Drive backup.

1. Data We Collect

Data TypeWhere / HowPurposeLegal Basis
Transactions, accounts, categories, budgets, tags Local database — on your device Core app functionality Contract performance
Advertising identifier (AAID) Google AdMob SDK Ad serving, frequency capping, measurement Legitimate interest
Crash data (stack trace, device model, OS) Firebase Crashlytics Debugging Legitimate interest
Anonymous usage events Firebase Analytics Feature usage measurement Legitimate interest
Purchase state Google Play Billing Pro subscription verification Contract performance
Google account + Drive appDataFolder Google Sign-In + Drive SDK (optional) Backup / restore Explicit consent

We do not collect: name/surname, email, phone number, address, location, contacts, photos, health data, bank account numbers.

2. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Drive API — policies.google.com/privacy

3. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local data: Removed when you uninstall the app. Can be manually deleted via Settings > "Factory reset".
  • Drive backup: You can delete it from your Drive account via appDataFolder.
  • Firebase: Analytics 14 months (default), Crashlytics 90 days.
  • AdMob: Subject to Google's retention policies.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete your data, and object to its processing. For requests, please contact sehappdev@gmail.com.

5. Children

This app is not directed at children under 13; we do not knowingly collect children's data.

6. International Transfers

Data originating from Google services may be transferred to the United States and other Google data centers. Google operates under the Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) and the Data Privacy Framework (DPF).

7. Ad Consent

For EU / EEA / UK / Switzerland users, consent is requested on first launch via the Google UMP (User Messaging Platform) SDK. You can manage your ad preferences at any time from your device's Google settings (Settings > Google > Ads).

8. Changes

The "last updated" date at the top of this page is refreshed when the policy changes.
